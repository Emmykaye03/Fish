About Us

Early in Suburban’s history the need for greater social and charitable activity became apparent. On April 8, 1919, Suburban Social Club was formed and quickly became the leader in creating a social life for the lodge, its members, and their families with major programs and outings.
The faithful members of the Social Club kept the fish fries going all year long enabling us to pay off the full indebtedness of for our new lodge building in only four years. They continue working every Saturday from March through November to support the lodge and many charities.
